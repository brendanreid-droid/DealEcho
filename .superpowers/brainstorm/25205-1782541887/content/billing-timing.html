When does billing update for extra seats?

Manager adds a 6th user ($13/mo extra) — when does Stripe charge them?

A

Immediately (prorated)

Stripe charges the prorated amount for the rest of the billing period right away. Next invoice reflects the new seat count. Most transparent — user sees the charge instantly.

B

Next billing cycle

Extra seat activates immediately but billing only changes on the next renewal. Simpler UX — no surprise mid-cycle charges. Slightly favors the customer.
